Refund Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we will refund your payment in full. No questions asked.

Our Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of every product in the Spencer Wealth Series. We want you to feel confident in your purchase, which is why we offer a straightforward, no-hassle refund policy.

How It Works

  1. If you are unsatisfied with your purchase for any reason, simply contact us within 30 days of your purchase date.
  2. Let us know you would like a refund. No explanation is required.
  3. We will process your full refund within 5-7 business days.

Eligibility

  • Refund requests must be made within 30 days of the original purchase date.
  • The refund applies to the full purchase amount of the tier you bought.
  • Each customer is eligible for one refund per product purchase.

Digital Product Nature

The Spencer Wealth Series products are digital goods (ebooks, spreadsheets, and guides) delivered electronically. Because digital products cannot be "returned" in the traditional sense, our 30-day guarantee is designed to give you ample time to review the material and decide if it is right for you.

Upon receiving a refund, your license to use the purchased materials is revoked.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us through our website with your purchase details (order number, email used at checkout, and date of purchase). We will respond within 24-48 hours and initiate your refund promptly.

Late Requests

Refund requests received after the 30-day window has passed will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis, but are not guaranteed.
